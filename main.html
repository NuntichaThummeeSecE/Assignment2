Our Story

At Thai Spice, we bring the heart of Thailand to your plate. Founded in 2012, our mission is to share the rich flavors and culinary traditions of Thai cuisine with our community. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients, creating dishes that are both authentic and innovative.

Discover Our Menu

From classic Pad Thai to aromatic Green Curry, our menu offers a variety of dishes to satisfy every palate. Explore our appetizers, entrees, and desserts.

Green Curry

A creamy coconut milk-based curry infused with green curry paste, bamboo shoots, and your choice of protein, served with jasmine rice.

Pad Thai

Savor our Pad Thai—stir-fried noodles with veggies, eggs, and your choice of protein. Tossed in tamarind sauce and topped with peanuts and lime. Delicious!

Tom Yum Kung

A fragrant and spicy soup with shrimp, mushrooms, and fresh herbs. A must-try for spice lovers!

Mango Sticky Rice

A traditional Thai dessert made with sweet sticky rice, ripe mango, and drizzled with coconut cream.
